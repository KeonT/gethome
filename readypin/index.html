Why this?

Sometimes, being a night owl has its downsides; the night is dark, and full of terrors. Let us get rid of them for you.

Companionship

Safety in numbers is no joke. Bonus points for ensuring your walking partner is verified by the campus. Even more points for pairing up users in close proximity with one another, looking to travel in the same direction.

Tech Powered

We use the latest technology, ranging from Google's precise geolocation to Cisco Meraki's precise heatmap and tracking software. It's not just for show though; these different pieces of software come together to ensure you reach your destination safely and efficiently.

Privacy

With all the talk about tracking and path-finding, it's hard to not feel worried about one's privacy. No worries, we understand. Your personal information is never saved for over 10 minutes, and you and your partner's destinations will remain anonymous to one another, as will your original location - we'll have you meet up at a neutral, popular location and commence your journey home from there.

About

We are a team of proficient hackers with an insatiable desire to learn and do more. In this single project alone, one can already see our willingness to expand and learn through the wide variety of API usage in an attempt to further round our abilities and delve deeper into the world of computer science. We hope this Cal Hacks 4.0 projects can inspire and motivate you to reach for the stars as well.
